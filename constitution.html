TALSU Constitution

The official governing constitution of the Tangaza Local Government Students Union (TALSU), Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto Chapter.

United We Stand, Divided We Fall
Preamble
"We the Tangaza local government students of Sokoto state (indigene) give ourselves a unique identity, with the interest of promoting and protecting our noble professions and local government hereby put forward this constitution as a guide to our activities."
Drafted: 2017/2018 Academic Session · President: Abubakar Umar · Sec. Gen: Adamu Umar
